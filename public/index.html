Join the fight against cancer
One patient, one sequence, one solution

IMMUNOMARK is a project dedicated to advancing cancer research through new technologies and immunotherapy. Every euro you donate will go entirely to support this research.

Since 2019, we have included more than 400 patients in our study. Our goal is to increase this number ONE MORE with each donation. This will allow us to analyse molecular information and decipher the keys to the use of immunotherapy in the fight against cancer. Collaborating in this cause brings research closer to clinical practice and supports the use of precision medicine. And above all, it fosters real hope for patients and their families.

About us

Translational Research in Cancer Immunotherapy

We are a team committed to science and innovation, dedicated to discovering new solutions in the fight against cancer. Our team is made up of highly qualified professionals, including oncologists, laboratory technicians and scientists specialised in bioinformatics and genomics. We are located in the Medical Oncology UGCI of the Regional and Virgen de la Victoria University Hospitals.

IMMUNOMARK

The IMMUNOMARK project focuses on the integration of molecular analyzes to identify predictive biomarkers of response to immunotherapy against lung cancer and metastatic melanoma, using both solid and liquid biopsies. It covers analysis at the genomic (DNA mutations), transcriptomic (gene expression) and epigenomic (regulation of gene expression) level.

To date, the project has contributed significantly to the field of precision immunotherapy, developing new tools and methodologies to predict treatment efficacy. Here we present some publications in international magazines. With this fundraising we intend to continue including patients in our study and broaden the scope to include patients with other types of cancer.

Solidary race

Our cause IMMUNOMARK: ONE MORE has been selected by RealBuzz as a charity associated with the Copenhagen 2024 Half Marathon. Dubbed the 'Home of Charity Running', RealBuzz provides resources and support for runners and charities, with the goal of maximizing the impact of donations raised through running activities.

Being represented in this race will allow us to break borders and take our cause to an international level. Furthermore, connecting support for scientific research with the running community leads to greater social impact, uniting science and sport.

Charity

All fundraising will be managed by the WhyDonate platform. Once completed, the proceeds will be transferred to the Andalusian Public Foundation for Malaga Research in Biomedicine and Health, managing entity of the Malaga Biomedical Research Institute (IBIMA) and Nanomedicine Platform. The donation will be used in the active project of the group belonging to the Intercenter Clinical Management Unit of Medical Oncology of Malaga, specifically the Translational Research in Cancer Immunotherapy group (CIMO-02).
